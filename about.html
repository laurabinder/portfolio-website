About me

My Story

I have been working successfully and enthusiastically as a designer for six years - but am now actively seeking a change to a new field of work with a wider spectrum. As a designer, I have always been looking for processes and solutions that focus on user needs as well as aesthetics.

This has led me to actively move into UI / UX design, where I can bring my experience with independent creation of company brandings and the corresponding websites, single shop components as well as creation of product lines. On a classical digital graphic basis, I have started to build up professional UX knowledge through further training at CareerFoundry. Thus, in addition to very in-depth knowledge in the design of user-centred interfaces, I also have knowledge in areas such as research, target group identification or the creation of navigation structures. I bring valuable skills such as strong communication, systemic and critical thinking, and problem-solving skills to my work. Now I'm looking forward to applying my new skills in combination with my previous knowledge and helping others turn their ideas into products that are pleasant to use and visually appealing. I currently live and work in Kiel, but am available for both freelance and contract work remotely.
