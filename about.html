About me

My Story

I have been working successfully and enthusiastically as a designer for six years - but am now actively seeking a change to a new field of work with a wider spectrum. With my successfully completed further training as a UX designer, I would like to change professionally and apply my learned skills.

On a classical-graphic basis, I have started to build up professional UX knowledge, among other things, through further training at CareerFoundry. Thus, in addition to very in-depth knowledge in the design of user-centred interfaces, I also have knowledge in areas such as research, target group identification or the creation of navigation structures. My personal motivation for this further development towards the field of UX design is that as a designer I have processes and solutions that, in addition to the aesthetic side, also take into account the needs of the customer or user. the needs of the customer or user.
