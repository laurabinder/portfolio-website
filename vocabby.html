Challenges

„When I read texts in technical English for my work, I want to learn words in their context and with further explanations, so I can make sure I‘m not using them incorrectly.“

„When I get a specialist book in English, I want to have a set of the appropriate technical terms available, so that I can learn the technical jargon while reading it.“

Solution

We believe that by focusing the learning process on planned events in the future and by giving assistance with the time planning for Benjamin, we will achieve that he continues his learning until the planned event and does not lose focus and gives up.
